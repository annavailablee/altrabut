Who We Are

A trusted name in Bahrain's food trade

Al Trabut Trading & Marketing Co. W.L.L., known commercially as Trade Link, has established itself as a reliable partner for food product distribution across the Kingdom of Bahrain. We operate across import, wholesale, and retail — covering frozen foods, packaged goods, and a broad range of food and non-food items.


Backed by years of market experience and a strong regional network, we connect quality suppliers with businesses and consumers throughout Bahrain.
